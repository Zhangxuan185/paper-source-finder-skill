# Ranking Systems

Venue ranking depends on field. Never mix frameworks casually.

## Computer Science

Common frameworks:

- `CCF`
- `CORE`

Typical use:

- CS conferences and journals
- AI / ML / NLP / vision / systems / databases / HCI

## Management / Information Systems / Business

Common frameworks:

- `ABS`
- `FT50`

Typical use:

- MIS journals
- management journals
- business school evaluation contexts

## Journal Metric Systems

Common frameworks:

- `JCR quartile`
- `Scopus / CiteScore quartile`
- field-specific national or institutional systems

Use these only when they truly apply to the journal and the user accepts that framework.

## Rules

1. Always name the framework.
2. If the field is unclear, do not guess.
3. `CCF A` and `JCR Q1` are not interchangeable.
4. Conference rankings and journal quartiles are not the same thing.

## Good output examples

- `Ranking: CCF A (Computer Science)`
- `Ranking: CORE A*`
- `Ranking: ABS 4`
- `Ranking: JCR Q1`
- `Ranking: unknown (framework not verified)`
